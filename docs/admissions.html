Admissions

Admission Guidelines

  • Admissions open from March every year for PreKG to Grade XII.
  • Application forms available online and at school office.
  • Entrance test/interviews required for admissions in higher grades.
  • Documents needed: Birth Certificate, Previous year marksheet, Community Certificate.

Academic Calendar

  • First term: June - September
  • Second term: October - December
  • Third term: January - April
